# Provider Capabilities

## Music Providers
| Provider | Difference | Vocals | Real-time |
|----------|------------|--------|-----------|
| **Lyria** (Google) | Optimized for speed and high-quality instrumental music. | ❌ | ✅ |
| **MiniMax** (AIMLAPI) | Supports vocal generation and lyrics integration. Slower, async processing. | ✅ | ❌ |

## Video Providers
| Provider | Capabilities | Image-to-Video |
|----------|--------------|----------------|
| **Veo** (Google) | Fast, high-quality video generation from prompts. | ✅ |
| **Kling** (KlingAI) | Cinematic quality, longer durations (not used in this challenge). | ✅ |

### Vocal Support
The **MiniMax** provider (via AIMLAPI) is the primary engine for generating songs with lyrics and vocals.
